Final Jeopardy — The Big Picture
This is the maximum Non-Economic Loss award for a living claimant with no Economic Loss — and the threshold above which a prior lawsuit settlement or VCF1 award would result in zero additional compensation from the VCF.
What is $340,000?

The maximum NEL award is $340,000. If a claimant previously received a settlement or VCF1 award equal to or greater than this amount and has no Economic Loss, the collateral offset results in a $0 award. This is why prior lawsuit history requires attorney review before investing resources in building the claim.
